Our Mission

At DetectYou, we believe everyone deserves to feel secure online. Our mission is to democratize access to advanced identity verification tools and provide comprehensive education about digital privacy and safety.

We understand that the digital landscape can be overwhelming and dangerous for many users. That's why we've created a suite of easy-to-use detection tools that help verify identities, check information authenticity, and protect against online threats.

What We Offer

  • Advanced face detection and analysis tools
  • Email verification and security checking
  • Username investigation across platforms
  • Text analysis and similarity detection
  • Comprehensive privacy and safety education

Our Commitment to Privacy

We take your privacy seriously. All our demo tools are designed for educational purposes and do not store, transmit, or process your personal data. Your security and privacy are our top priorities.
